It is one act, but there is more
SIR HARRY.

The sword will lend it an air.

JOEL.

[_In a low voice, to_ ELLEN.] His screws are droppin' out. Come and pack. We've got to mark time and skip.

JOEL.

[_Behind his hand._] His top-loft leaks!
